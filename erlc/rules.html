ERLC Roleplay Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP

All roleplays must maintain a realistic standard. Avoid any actions that could be considered unrealistic.

Rule 2 | VDM

Vehicle Deathmatch is not allowed. Do not ram or shoot at vehicles without a valid reason.

Rule 3 | RDM

Random Deathmatch is not allowed. You may not shoot or kill anyone without valid in-game reasoning.

Rule 4 | Gun Motion

Use motions like “grabs gun” or “slings” when pulling any weapon except for pistols or tasers. Required only when pulling on law enforcement.

Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars

Your in-game avatar must appear realistic. Morphs or abnormal character features are not allowed.

Rule 6 | New Life Rule (NLR)

After dying or being arrested, your character must forget all previous memories and events.

Rule 7 | Team Creation

Creating unofficial teams like CIA/FBI is not allowed. Join through the Discord application process.

Rule 8 | Realistic Driving & Crashes

Drive realistically at all times and roleplay every crash regardless of severity.

Rule 9 | Fear RP

Always fear for your life. If someone points a gun at you, follow their demands realistically.

Rule 10 | Detain Requests

Always accept detain requests. If the arrest feels unfair, report it to staff afterwards.

Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying

Do not carry firearms around just to show off. This can lead to misunderstandings and moderation.

Rule 12 | Cop Baiting & Yielding

Do not try to get the police's attention without reason. Always yield to emergency vehicles.

Rule 13 | Robberies

Wait at least 5 minutes between robberies. Staff approval is required before robbing the bank.

Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays

Do not interfere with ongoing roleplays or create chaos in someone else's scene.

Rule 15 | Priorities

No criminal activity is allowed while a priority is active.

Rule 16 | Pointless Pursuits

You must have a reason to run from police — don't start pursuits just for fun.

Rule 17 | Group Limits

Criminal groups are limited to 4 people. No more than 3 may rob the jewelry store together.

Rule 18 | SWAT Gear

Only trained SWAT members may use SWAT weapons, gear, and vehicles.

Rule 19 | VC (Voice Chat)

If the session is VC-based, you must be in a Discord VC with a working mic when you load in.

Rule 20 | Safe Areas

No crimes may be committed in Safe Zones like Fire Station, LEO spawns, or DOT station.

Rule 21 | Uniforms & Liveries

Use only official department uniforms and liveries when playing in our server.

Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff Cars

Pull over or stop if a staff vehicle with lights is trying to stop you.

Rule 23 | Display Names

Roleplay names must be realistic and appropriate. No impersonation or staff-related names.

Rule 24 | Respawning

Call EMS when downed. Wait 2 minutes before respawning. If LE is involved, wait for a mod to heal you for custody.

*Just because a rule isn’t listed does not mean it won't apply to you*
