ERLC Staff Guidelines

1 | Professionalism
Staff must remain professional at all times while representing the server, both in-game and in Discord.
2 | Abuse of Power
Staff may not use commands, permissions, or tools for personal gain or revenge. Abuse will result in removal.
3 | Transparency
All significant actions (e.g., bans, kicks, scene removals) must be logged with an explanation.
4 | Fairness
Treat every player equally regardless of their rank, status, or personal relationship with you.
5 | No Bias
Avoid favoritism. Do not let friendships influence moderation decisions.
6 | Internal Disputes
Staff disagreements must be taken to leadership privately — never in public chats or in-game.
7 | Responsiveness
Answer support tickets and assist players as quickly and efficiently as possible.
8 | Command Use
Only use moderation commands when necessary and justified. Do not test commands in live sessions.
9 | Inactivity
Extended inactivity must be reported to the Head Staff. Inactive staff may be demoted.
10 | Roleplay Participation
Staff are expected to roleplay realistically when not actively moderating. Set a good example.
11 | Confidentiality
Internal discussions, reports, and moderation logs are confidential and may not be leaked.
12 | Chain of Command
Follow the staff hierarchy. If unsure how to handle a situation, escalate it appropriately.
*Staff are held to a higher standard — misconduct may result in instant removal depending on severity.*
